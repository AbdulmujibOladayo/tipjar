Integration

Two lines. That is all.

Drop it anywhere

Load the script once, place the tag where you want the widget, and TipJar handles the rest. Works in any static site, Ghost blog, Notion embed, or custom web app.

<script type="module" src="https://cdn.tipjar.dev/widget.js" ></script> <tip-jar to="GABCD...XYZ" suggested="1,5,20" ></tip-jar>
How it works

From embed to payout in seconds

1

Add the snippet

Paste two lines into any page. Hugo, Ghost, Notion embed, or plain HTML. If it renders in a browser, TipJar works there.

2

Fan picks an amount

They choose from your suggested amounts or type a custom one. One tap to confirm. No account required, no wallet install.

3

USDC lands in your wallet

Stellar settles in 3 to 5 seconds. Your balance updates before the fan even closes the tab.
